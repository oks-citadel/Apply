## Tone & Voice Guidelines

**Professional but Approachable**
- Use clear, jargon-free language
- Be conversational but not overly casual
- Professional without being stuffy

**Confident but Not Arrogant**
- Share results and data authentically
- Acknowledge challenges in job searching
- Position as helper, not savior

**Helpful and Empowering**
- Focus on user success and benefits
- Provide actionable advice
- Celebrate user wins

**Clear and Concise**
- Get to the point quickly
- Use short sentences and paragraphs
- Break up text with bullets and formatting

**Action-Oriented**
- Strong CTAs throughout
- Encourage next steps
- Create urgency without pressure
