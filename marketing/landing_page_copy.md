## FAQ Section

### Question 1: How does ApplyforUs apply to jobs on my behalf?

We integrate with major job boards like Indeed, LinkedIn, ZipRecruiter, and company career pages. Our AI reads job application forms and fills them out using your profile information. For each application, we customize your resume to match the job description and generate a tailored cover letter. You can review applications before they're submitted or let automation handle everything.

### Question 2: Will employers know I used automation?

No. Applications submitted through ApplyforUs look identical to manual applications. We use human-like timing patterns and customize each submission. Many of our users report higher response rates because our AI optimizes resumes for ATS systems better than manual applications.

### Question 3: How do you ensure application quality?

We use multiple quality checks: AI verification of form completion, resume relevance scoring, and duplicate detection. You can set approval workflows to review applications before submission. Our system learns from your feedback to improve accuracy over time. You maintain full control over what gets submitted.

### Question 4: Can I pause or stop applications at any time?

Absolutely. You can pause, resume, or stop applications with one click. You can also adjust preferences, exclude specific companies, or change your daily application limit anytime. Your account settings give you complete control over the automation.

### Question 5: What job boards and platforms do you support?

We integrate with 50+ platforms including Indeed, LinkedIn, Glassdoor, ZipRecruiter, Monster, CareerBuilder, AngelList, and thousands of company career pages. We're constantly adding new integrations. If there's a platform you need, let us know and we'll prioritize it.

### Question 6: Is my personal information secure?

Yes. We use bank-level AES-256 encryption, are SOC 2 Type II certified, and comply with GDPR and CCPA regulations. Your data is never sold or shared with third parties without your explicit consent. You can export or delete your data anytime. Read our [Privacy Policy] for complete details.

### Question 7: How does the AI resume optimization work?

Our AI analyzes the job description to identify key skills, qualifications, and requirements. It then reorders and highlights relevant sections of your resume, incorporates important keywords naturally, and adjusts formatting for ATS compatibility. You always maintain control and can edit any changes before submission.

### Question 8: Can I use this while currently employed?

Yes. Many users job search while employed. We offer privacy features like scheduling applications during off-hours, excluding your current employer, and discreet notifications. Your job search activity is completely private unless you choose to share it.

### Question 9: What if I want to apply manually to some companies?

You have full flexibility. You can exclude specific companies from automation, apply manually through our dashboard, or mix automated and manual applications. Many users automate high-volume applications while manually handling dream companies.

### Question 10: Do you offer refunds?

Yes. We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If you're not satisfied with ApplyforUs for any reason, contact support within 30 days of purchase for a full refund. No questions asked.
