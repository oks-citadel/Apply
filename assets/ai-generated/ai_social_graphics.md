# AI Social Media Graphics Specifications
## ApplyforUs Platform

---

## Overview

Social media graphics are essential for brand awareness, user engagement, and community building. These specifications ensure consistent, platform-optimized, and effective visual content across all social channels.

## Platform Specifications

### LinkedIn

#### LinkedIn Post Image
- **Dimensions**: 1200x627px (1.91:1 ratio)
- **File Format**: PNG or JPG
- **File Size**: < 5MB
- **Safe Zone**: 40px padding from edges

#### LinkedIn Article Header
- **Dimensions**: 1200x627px
- **File Format**: PNG or JPG
- **File Size**: < 5MB

#### LinkedIn Video Cover
- **Dimensions**: 1200x628px
- **File Format**: JPG or PNG
- **File Size**: < 5MB

---

### Instagram

#### Instagram Feed Post (Square)
- **Dimensions**: 1080x1080px (1:1 ratio)
- **File Format**: JPG (recommended) or PNG
- **File Size**: < 30MB

#### Instagram Feed Post (Portrait)
- **Dimensions**: 1080x1350px (4:5 ratio)
- **File Format**: JPG or PNG
- **File Size**: < 30MB

#### Instagram Stories
- **Dimensions**: 1080x1920px (9:16 ratio)
- **File Format**: JPG, PNG, or MP4
- **File Size**: < 30MB (image), < 4GB (video)

- **Dimensions**: 1080x1920px (9:16 ratio)
- **File Format**: JPG
- **File Size**: < 1MB

## Brand Elements for Social Graphics

### Typography Guidelines

**Headlines**
- Font: Inter Bold or Montserrat Bold
- Size: 48-72pt (desktop), 36-48pt (mobile)
- Color: White on dark backgrounds, Brand Purple on light
- Line height: 1.2

**Body Text**
- Font: Inter Regular or Open Sans Regular
- Size: 24-36pt
- Color: White or dark gray depending on background
- Line height: 1.5

**Captions/Metadata**
- Font: Inter Medium
- Size: 18-24pt
- Color: Gray or muted purple

### Color Palette

**Primary**
- Brand Purple: #8B5CF6
- Brand Indigo: #4C1D95
- White: #FFFFFF
- Black: #000000

**Accents**
- Success Green: #10B981
- Warning Orange: #F97316
- Highlight Pink: #EC4899
- Info Blue: #3B82F6

**Gradients**
- Primary: #8B5CF6 to #4C1D95
- Secondary: #3B82F6 to #06B6D4
- Sunset: #F97316 to #EC4899
- Success: #10B981 to #059669

### Logo Usage

**Primary Logo**: Full color on white/light backgrounds
**Reverse Logo**: White on dark/purple backgrounds
**Icon Only**: When space is limited
**Minimum Size**: 80px width
**Clear Space**: 20px minimum around logo

## AI Generation Workflows

### Step 1: Concept & Brief
Define message, audience, platform, and desired action

### Step 2: Generate Base Image
Use Midjourney, DALL-E, or Stable Diffusion with detailed prompt

### Step 3: Add Text & Branding
Use Canva, Figma, or Photoshop to add typography and logo

### Step 4: Optimize for Platform
Export in correct dimensions, format, and file size

### Step 5: Create Variations
Generate multiple options for A/B testing

### Step 6: Schedule & Post
Use social media management tools
