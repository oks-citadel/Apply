# AI Video Content Scripts
## ApplyforUs Platform

---

## Overview

Video content is essential for explaining complex features, building trust, and increasing conversion rates. These scripts are designed for multiple distribution channels including homepage, social media, email campaigns, and paid advertising.

---

## Video Production Guidelines

### Technical Specifications

#### Resolution & Format
- **4K**: 3840x2160px (future-proof)
- **Full HD**: 1920x1080px (standard)
- **Vertical**: 1080x1920px (mobile/social)
- **Square**: 1080x1080px (social feeds)

#### Frame Rate
- **24 fps**: Cinematic feel
- **30 fps**: Standard video
- **60 fps**: Smooth motion (demos)

#### File Formats
- **Master**: MOV or MP4 (H.264)
- **Web**: MP4 (compressed)
- **Social**: MP4 (platform-optimized)

#### Audio Specifications
- **Sample Rate**: 48kHz
- **Bit Depth**: 24-bit
- **Format**: WAV (master), MP3/AAC (delivery)
- **Loudness**: -14 LUFS (streaming standard)

### Script: "The Job Search Revolution"

windows showing job applications. Overwhelmed expression. Clock on wall
shows late night. Coffee cups stacked. Papers scattered.

Camera: Close-up then pull back to reveal chaos
Lighting: Dim, stressed atmosphere
Music: Tense, building
```

**Voiceover**
```
"Job searching is exhausting. Hundreds of applications. Endless forms.
Zero responses."
```

**On-Screen Text**

"The traditional job search is broken"
[Fades in at 2 seconds, fades out at 5 seconds]

## 3. 2-Minute Product Demo

### Goal

### Distribution
- Sales presentations

- Demo requests
- Product page
- YouTube channel

**Visual**
```
Professional host on camera or voiceover with screen recording
ApplyforUs homepage shown briefly
Transition to demo account dashboard
```

**Voiceover**

"Welcome to ApplyforUs, the AI-powered job search platform that finds and
applies to your dream jobs automatically. I'm going to walk you through
every feature in the next two minutes. Let's dive in."

---

#### Section 1: Sign Up & Onboarding (15-35 seconds)

**Visual**
```
Screen recording:
- Sign-up page (Google OAuth option highlighted)
- Welcome screen with Pilot AI assistant
- Quick onboarding questionnaire
- Resume upload with progress bar

```

**Voiceover**
```
"Getting started is simple. Sign up with email or Google in seconds. Our AI
assistant, Pilot, guides you through onboarding. Upload your resume, and

---

#### Section 2: Profile Setup & Preferences (35-55 seconds)

**Visual**
```
Screen recording:
- Profile completion interface
- Job preferences panel (location, remote, salary range, job types)
- Skills tagging with AI suggestions

- Industry preferences
- Company culture fit quiz

**Voiceover**
```
"Customize your job search preferences: location, remote work options, salary
requirements, and more. Our AI suggests skills based on your experience and
helps you define your ideal role. Set how aggressively you want to apply -
from a few carefully selected positions to dozens per day."
```

---

#### Section 3: AI Job Matching (55-75 seconds)

**Visual**
```
Screen recording:
- Dashboard with recommended jobs feed
- Job card details (match score, reasons, salary, remote)
- Clicking into job detail view
- Match explanation panel (why this job matches)

- Company profile information

**Voiceover**
```
"Your dashboard shows AI-matched jobs ranked by compatibility. Each listing

recommendations."

---

#### Section 4: Resume & Cover Letter Customization (75-95 seconds)

**Visual**
```
Screen recording:

**Voiceover**
```

experience and keywords from the job description. See exactly what changes
are made. We also generate personalized cover letters based on the role,
company, and your background. Edit if you want, or approve and move on."
```

---

**Visual**
```
Screen recording:

- Toggle switch activation

- Blacklist companies feature

- Success/failure tracking

**Voiceover**
```
"Enable Auto-Apply to let AI submit applications 24/7 on your behalf. Set

application limits. Blacklist specific companies if needed. Monitor real-time
activity logs and get notifications for every application submitted and every

```

---

```

**Voiceover**

### Production Notes

## Video Production Checklist

### Pre-Production
- [ ] Script finalized and approved
- [ ] Storyboard created
- [ ] Shot list prepared
- [ ] Voiceover artist booked (if needed)
- [ ] Location scouted (if on-camera)
- [ ] Props and equipment ready
- [ ] Demo account prepared with sample data
- [ ] Screen recording software tested

### Production
- [ ] High-quality video recording (4K if possible)
- [ ] Clean audio recording (separate track)
- [ ] Multiple takes captured
- [ ] B-roll footage captured
- [ ] Screen recordings smooth and clear
- [ ] On-camera talent comfortable and natural
- [ ] Lighting consistent and professional
- [ ] Background appropriate and non-distracting

### Post-Production
- [ ] Video edited to script timing
- [ ] Color correction applied
- [ ] Audio mixed and balanced
- [ ] Music added (licensed)
- [ ] Sound effects added (subtle)
- [ ] Text overlays and graphics added
- [ ] Transitions smooth and professional
- [ ] Captions/subtitles added (accessibility)
- [ ] Brand elements included (logo, colors)
- [ ] Call-to-action clear and prominent
- [ ] Multiple format exports (16:9, 9:16, 1:1)
- [ ] Compressed for web delivery

### Distribution
- [ ] YouTube upload with optimized title, description, tags
- [ ] Social media versions created
- [ ] Thumbnail designed and A/B tested
- [ ] Video embedded on website
- [ ] Email campaign version prepared
- [ ] Analytics tracking set up
- [ ] Launch scheduled
- [ ] Promotion plan ready
