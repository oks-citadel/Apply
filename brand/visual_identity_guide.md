# ApplyforUs Visual Identity Guide

## Brand Name Usage

### Official Name
**ApplyforUs**
- Single word, no spaces
- Capital A, capital F, capital U
- No hyphens or special characters
- Never: Apply For Us, Applyforus, apply4us, ApplyForUs

### Acceptable Variations

**Full Name in Text:**
"ApplyforUs" - Standard usage in body text and headings

**Logo Mark:**
Can use stylized version with custom typography (see Logo Guidelines)

**URL/Technical:**
- Domain: applyforus.com
- Email: @applyforus.com
- Social handles: @applyforus (lowercase acceptable for technical constraints)

**First Reference in Documents:**
"ApplyforUs™" (include trademark symbol in formal documents, marketing materials)

**Subsequent References:**
- "ApplyforUs" or "the platform"
- Avoid: "we" when referring to the product (use for the company)

### Incorrect Usage
- ❌ Apply For Us
- ❌ apply for us
- ❌ Applyforus
- ❌ ApplyForUs
- ❌ Apply4Us
- ❌ AFU

## Logo Usage Guidelines

### Primary Logo
The primary logo consists of:
- **Wordmark**: "ApplyforUs" in custom typography
- **Symbol**: Abstract arrow/path icon representing forward movement
- **Configuration**: Symbol left, wordmark right (horizontal layout)

### Logo Variations

**1. Primary Horizontal Logo**
- Use for: Website headers, marketing materials, presentations
- Minimum width: 120px digital, 1.5 inches print
- Clear space: Minimum 20% of logo height on all sides

**2. Stacked Logo**
- Use for: Square social media avatars, mobile apps, small spaces
- Symbol above wordmark
- Minimum width: 60px digital, 0.75 inches print

**3. Symbol Only (Icon)**
- Use for: Favicons, app icons, very small applications
- Minimum size: 16px × 16px digital
- Always maintain aspect ratio

**4. Wordmark Only**
- Use for: Text-heavy contexts, partnerships where symbol might confuse
- Use sparingly; prefer full logo when possible

### Color Variations

**Primary (Color on White)**
- Symbol: Primary Blue (#4F46E5)
- Wordmark: Neutral Dark (#0F172A)
- Background: White (#FFFFFF)
- **Use for**: Primary digital applications, light backgrounds

**Reversed (White on Color)**
- Symbol and Wordmark: White (#FFFFFF)
- Background: Primary Blue (#4F46E5) or Neutral Dark (#0F172A)
- **Use for**: Dark mode, dark backgrounds, colored buttons

**Monochrome Dark**
- All elements: Neutral Dark (#0F172A)
- **Use for**: Print (newspaper, fax), grayscale contexts

**Monochrome Light**
- All elements: White (#FFFFFF)
- **Use for**: White on dark backgrounds, dark mode

### Clear Space Requirements
Maintain minimum clear space around logo equal to the height of the "A" in "ApplyforUs"
- No text, graphics, or other elements within clear space
- Extends on all four sides
- Critical for maintaining visual impact and legibility

### Size Requirements

**Digital Minimum Sizes:**
- Horizontal logo: 120px width
- Stacked logo: 60px width
- Icon only: 16px × 16px

**Print Minimum Sizes:**
- Horizontal logo: 1.5 inches width
- Stacked logo: 0.75 inches width
- Icon only: 0.25 inches × 0.25 inches

### Usage Don'ts

**Never:**
- ❌ Rotate the logo
- ❌ Change colors outside approved palette
- ❌ Distort or stretch logo (always maintain aspect ratio)
- ❌ Add effects (shadows, glows, gradients) unless specified
- ❌ Place on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
- ❌ Outline the logo
- ❌ Rearrange elements (symbol and wordmark)
- ❌ Use old logo versions

### Background Usage

**Safe Backgrounds:**
- ✅ White (#FFFFFF)
- ✅ Light Gray (#F8FAFC, #F1F5F9)
- ✅ Primary Blue (#4F46E5) with white logo
- ✅ Neutral Dark (#0F172A) with white logo
- ✅ Subtle gradients (see approved gradients)

**Requires Testing:**
- ⚠️ Photography (ensure adequate contrast)
- ⚠️ Patterns (may need white/dark background shape)
- ⚠️ Accent colors (test legibility)

**Avoid:**
- ❌ Busy patterns without solid background shape
- ❌ Low-contrast combinations
- ❌ Multiple bright colors competing with logo

### Color Usage Guidelines

**Hierarchy:**
1. Use Primary Blue for most important actions and brand elements
2. Use Primary Purple for secondary importance and AI-related features
3. Use neutral colors for text and backgrounds
4. Use accent colors sparingly for emphasis

**Accessibility:**
- All text colors must meet WCAG AA standards (4.5:1 for normal text, 3:1 for large text)
- Primary Blue (#4F46E5) on white meets AA for large text only
- Neutral Dark (#0F172A) on white meets AAA standards
- Test all color combinations before deployment

**States:**
- Hover: Darken by 10-15% or increase saturation
- Active/Pressed: Darken by 20-25%
- Disabled: Use Neutral Gray (#94A3B8) at 50% opacity
- Focus: 2px outline in Primary Blue with 2px offset

## Typography

- **Personality**: Modern, clean, highly legible, tech-forward

## Accessibility Guidelines

### Color Contrast
- **WCAG AA Minimum**: 4.5:1 for normal text, 3:1 for large text (18px+)
- **WCAG AAA Goal**: 7:1 for normal text, 4.5:1 for large text
- Test all color combinations with contrast checker
- Never use color alone to convey information

### Typography Accessibility
- Minimum body text size: 16px
- Maximum line length: 75 characters
- Line height: Minimum 1.5 for body text
- Paragraph spacing: At least 2× font size
- Letter spacing: At least 0.12× font size for body text

### Interactive Elements
- Minimum touch target: 44px × 44px
- Focus indicators: 2px solid Primary Blue, 2px offset
- Hover states must be visible
- Disabled states must be clear (50% opacity)

### Motion & Animation
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` media query
- Animations should enhance, not distract
- Maximum animation duration: 300ms for UI, 500ms for page transitions
- Provide option to disable animations
